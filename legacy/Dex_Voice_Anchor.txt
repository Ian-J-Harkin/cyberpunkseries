3. THE VOICE ANCHOR (Dex / Agent J Persona)
This section prevents "narrative drift" during drafting.

Narration Style: "Elmore Leonard writing Will Smith."

Sentences: Short and punchy during action; long and observational when Dex is bored or waiting.

The "Unimpressed" Filter: Dex is never intimidated by high technology or "dystopian" scale. He notices the scuffed shoes of a corporate enforcer before he notices the heavy weaponry.

Psychological Snapshot (ESTP 7w8): He moves toward fascination rather than fear. When faced with absurdity, he uses tactical wit to negotiate his way through rather than brooding.
