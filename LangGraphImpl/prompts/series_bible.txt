SERIES BIBLE — "Warm Neon" (Global Context, injected into every node)

NORTH STAR (Governance):
This is a world of low-grade, systemic collapse. Not apocalyptic — the lights are still on — but the bureaucracy is exhausted and the infrastructure is fraying at every seam. The tone is functional noir: problems get solved, but always sideways, always with friction.

WORLD COMPASS (Boundaries):
- NORTH (Earthside Ceiling): The corporate towers and regulatory bodies. Clean surfaces, impossible queues, biometric systems that don't quite work.
- SOUTH (The Underside): Street-level markets, fixers, analogue solutions. Where things actually get done.
- EAST (The Sprawl): Mid-ring residential and commercial zones. Neon-lit decay. Everything is slightly broken.
- WEST (Institutional Infrastructure): Hospitals, transit hubs, ID processing centres. The machinery of the state, grinding slowly.
- [VAULT — LOCKED]: The Moon's true nature (Computational Drift / Mineral Euphoria) is classified. Until vault_access_level >= 1, the Moon is referenced only as "Industrial Rumors."

TONE RULES:
1. 80/20 Rule: 80% of the world functions. 20% is visibly broken at all times.
2. No dystopian monologuing. The world is just Tuesday.
3. Bureaucracy is the antagonist, not a villain — it is indifferent, not malevolent.
4. The Fraying is ambient: a flickering sign, a kiosk that needs three restarts, a form that requires a form to request it.

VOCABULARY QUARANTINE (words that must NEVER appear):
- shimmering, chrome, hacker, cyberpunk, dystopia, neon (as an adjective for anything other than literal neon signs), matrix, neural-lace, jacked in, cyberspace, wetware
