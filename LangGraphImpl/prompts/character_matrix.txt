CHARACTER MATRIX — "Warm Neon" (Global Context, injected into every node)

PROTAGONIST — DEX
- MBTI: ESTP | Enneagram: 7w8
- Voice Anchor: Elmore Leonard meets Will Smith. Short declarative sentences. Notices shoes first, faces second. No internal brooding — always in motion.
- Physical: Fit but pragmatic. Nothing decorative.
- Arc Driver: Constant forward movement interrupted by systemic friction. Dex does not pause to reflect; he pivots.

SUPPORTING CAST — Character Matrix

NAME: Sarge

- Noticing Rule: Sarge notices the protagonist's biometric errors before anything else.
- Physical Anchor: Scuffed cybernetic arm, always tapping a surface. Never fully at rest.
- Trust Dynamic: Trust earned through competence, not warmth. Slow to give, hard to lose once given.

NAME: Glimmer
- Internal Frequency: Hyper-observant despite physical limitation. Processes rooms like a chess board.
- Noticing Rule: Glimmer notices social power dynamics — who defers to whom, who is lying.
- Physical Anchor: Cushion-propped autoimmunity. Ozone tea in a sealed flask. Never stands when she can sit; never sits on unpadded surfaces.
- Trust Dynamic: High baseline curiosity toward the protagonist. Cautious with information but generous with access.

NAME: Ad-Man
- Internal Frequency: Neural-Scraped Sloganeering. Thinks in product pitches; cannot help monetising every observation.
- Noticing Rule: Ad-Man notices what the protagonist is trying to sell — or avoid selling.
- Physical Anchor: Perpetually blinking ad-overlay visor. Gestures as if presenting to an invisible audience.
- Trust Dynamic: Transactional. Trust = perceived mutual benefit. Will flip if the deal changes.

RELATIONSHIP RULES:
- No character should deliver exposition unprompted.
